About Gaia Nutrition

Gaia Nutrition is a dedicated platform for promoting a healthy lifestyle through sustainable nutrition. We aim to empower individuals to make informed dietary choices that align with their personal needs and health goals.

Website Owner

Dr. Omar Mohammed Khalaf

Dr. Omar Mohammed Khalaf is a certified physician and nutrition specialist with over 15 years of experience in the field of nutrition and public health. He founded Gaia Nutrition with the goal of spreading health awareness and providing innovative, sustainable dietary solutions to the community. Dr. Khalaf holds a PhD in Clinical Nutrition from Cairo University and is passionate about helping individuals achieve their health goals through personalized nutrition plans.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform in providing sustainable nutrition solutions that enhance health and well-being for everyone.

Our Mission

To provide personalized nutrition plans and science-based health advice to improve the quality of life for our clients.
